Why this suite exists

Enterprise teams working with compliance, audit, e-invoicing, tax reporting and complex releases often lose time translating ambiguous source material into implementation-ready product artefacts. The result is slower discovery, inconsistent communication, weak auditability and higher delivery risk.

Hero case study — SAF-T PT + EU e-invoicing readiness

Context: A synthetic enterprise software company needs to assess a 20-page regulatory guidance document covering SAF-T PT validation, invoice data requirements and EU e-invoicing readiness.

Output: obligations, traceability matrix, validation rules, QA scenarios, Jira-ready items, customer-safe release notes, risk register and AI safety review.

Prototype benchmark hypothesis: reduce first-draft compliance/product analysis by 60–80% while preserving human review and source traceability.

Benchmark figures are portfolio targets based on synthetic workflows, not independently validated production claims.

The four-module system

Interpret — Compliance-to-Product Studio

Turns regulatory PDFs and guidance documents into obligation extraction, citation-verified requirements, traceability matrices, validation rules, QA scenarios and release notes.

Ideal for: Compliance, Product, Engineering, Audit, Risk.

Discover — Product Discovery Studio

Turns feature ideas into assumptions, trade-offs, RICE scoring, PM interview questions, Jira tickets, Gherkin acceptance criteria, MVP/V2 scope and quality gates.

Ideal for: Product Managers, Engineering Managers, Product Ops.

Communicate — Release Readiness Copilot

Creates customer-facing release notes, support notes, FAQs, QA checklists, known limitations, approval workflows and claim-safety rewrites.

Ideal for: Product Marketing, Support, Customer Success, QA.

Investigate — Decision Timeline Builder

Reconstructs product incidents into timelines, actor maps, decision audit trails, contradiction checks, risk registers and executive one-page briefs.

Ideal for: Product Ops, Engineering, Leadership, Support.

AI safety layer

  • Risk Score per output, including ambiguity and overclaiming risks.
  • Confidence Score for grounded outputs.
  • Human Review Required flags for compliance/legal interpretation.
  • Citation support checking against selected source chunks.
  • Grounding heatmap and source coverage to show what was and was not used.
  • Risky wording classifier for release communication.
  • Audit trail exports for review and replay.
