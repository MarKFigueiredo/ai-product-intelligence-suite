# AI Product Intelligence Suite 1.04

## AI product workflows for regulated enterprise environments

Transform complex regulatory documents, product decisions, release communication and incident timelines into auditable, human-reviewable product artefacts.

### Why this matters

Enterprise teams working with compliance-heavy software face slow discovery, high regulatory risk, inconsistent handoffs and weak auditability. This suite demonstrates how applied AI can accelerate those workflows without hiding the need for evidence, grounding and review.

## Four integrated workflows

### Interpret — Compliance-to-Product Studio

Upload a compliance document and generate obligations, product requirements, validation rules, QA test cases, release notes, traceability matrix, rule-based citation support checks and grounding heatmap.

### Discover — Product Discovery Studio

Convert ambiguous feature ideas into assumptions, validation plans, trade-offs, RICE scoring, Jira-style tickets, Gherkin acceptance criteria and QA matrices.

### Communicate — Release Readiness Copilot

Generate customer-safe release notes, internal support notes, claim safety review, risky wording rewrites and approval checklists.

### Investigate — Decision Timeline Builder

Reconstruct product incidents and escalations with timelines, actor maps, contradiction detection, risk registers, decision audit trails and executive briefs.

## Hero case study

A synthetic enterprise software company needs to evaluate SAF-T PT and EU e-invoicing guidance. The suite turns a sample regulatory document into an end-to-end product package: obligations → requirements → validation rules → QA → release communication → incident-ready audit trail.

## Responsible AI layer

- RAG and source chunk ranking
- Quote-level grounding
- Citation support scoring
- Human review flags

- Audit trail export
