# Release 1.02 — Companion payments playbook

1.02 is a small publication-focused update. It does not add another app module.

## Added

- `docs/COMPANION_PLAYBOOKS.md`
- `docs/AI_PAYMENTS_COMPLIANCE_MIT_COURSE_COMPANION.md`
- `companion_playbooks/ai_payments_compliance/Playbook_Draft_AI_Payments_Compliance_EN.docx`

- `sample_inputs/payments_iso20022_expected_outputs.json`

## Why

The payments playbook is useful as a third regulated domain and as a separate MIT course artifact, but it should not become another broad Streamlit module. Keeping it as a companion preserves scope discipline while demonstrating generalization and organizational implementation thinking.

The public app version is now `AI Product Intelligence Suite 1.02`.
