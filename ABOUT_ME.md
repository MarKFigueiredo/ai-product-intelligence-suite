## What I recently built

I developed the **AI Product Intelligence Suite**, a modular portfolio platform designed for the discovery → compliance → release → incident investigation cycle in compliance-heavy enterprise software.

It includes four integrated workflows:

- **Interpret — Compliance-to-Product Studio**  
  RAG, traceability matrix, rule-based citation support checks and human review flags.

- **Discover — Product Discovery Studio**  
  Assumptions, validation plans, trade-offs, RICE prioritization, Jira tickets, Gherkin acceptance criteria and QA matrices.

- **Communicate — Release Readiness Copilot**  
  Release notes, customer-safe messaging, claim safety review, risk scoring, support notes and approval checklists.

- **Investigate — Decision Timeline Builder**  
  Timelines, actor maps, contradiction detection, decision audit trails, postmortems and executive briefs.

This project consolidated my specialization in AI for regulated and complex enterprise environments, where rigor, auditability and safety are as important as speed.
